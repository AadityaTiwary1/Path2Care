About Skin Disease Detection

Our ML model has been trained on thousands of medical images to detect various skin conditions including:

Dataset reference: Skin Diseases Image Dataset (Kaggle)

  • Eczema: Inflammatory skin condition causing red, itchy patches
  • Melanoma: Serious form of skin cancer
  • Atopic Dermatitis: Chronic inflammatory skin disease
  • Basal Cell Carcinoma: Common type of skin cancer
  • Melanocytic Nevi: Moles and birthmarks
  • Benign Keratosis: Non-cancerous skin growths
  • Psoriasis: Autoimmune skin disorder
  • Seborrheic Keratoses: Benign skin tumors
  • Fungal Infections: Ringworm, candidiasis, and other fungal conditions
  • Viral Infections: Warts, molluscum, and related conditions
